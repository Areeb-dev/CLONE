About Metamorphosis

With rare marble rock, raw crystal formations and precious metals, the SKNUPS Metamorphosis Collection is a museum quality limited-edition drop.
Featuring supersized silhouettes this collection challenges the concept of their IRL counterparts, making the resulting items highly covetable art-piece collectibles.
These designs are inspired by a variety of specialist techniques such as gemology, stone-carving and wax-resist casting – all reflective of the savoir-faire qualities that underpin every collection by SKNUPS.
